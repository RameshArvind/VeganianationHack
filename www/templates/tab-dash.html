Animals are here with us, not for us

Creative vegan advocacy Through creative campaigns and powerful storytelling, we aim to create massive dialogue about animal rights and veganism.Read More.

Veganism is not just a diet or a lifestyle. It's a social justice movement aimed at liberating nonhuman animals from systematic oppression they go through.

We’re India’s most active animal rights network. We do leafleting, banner displays, protests, marches, documentary screenings and more.

Vegania believes in empowering activists to start grassroots movements. Through school and college clubs, we aim to empower the youth to take initiatives for the animals.
